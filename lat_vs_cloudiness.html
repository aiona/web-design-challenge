Weather Data Analysis


Latitude and Cloud Cover




The distribution of this plot is fairly amorphous. So, any relationship shown between latitude and the degree of cloud cover is weak. Linear regression analysis shows r values for Northern and Southern Hemispheres to be 0.30 and 0.25 respectively. While there are clusters of high and low cloud covered cities, these are distributed throughout the plot area.
